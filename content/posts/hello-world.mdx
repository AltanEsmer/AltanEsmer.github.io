Welcome to my corner of the internet. I've been meaning to start writing for a while, and this site is my attempt to finally make that happen. The plan is to write about the work I find interesting: web performance, distributed systems, developer tooling, and the occasional deep-dive into something I've been tinkering with on the side.

I'm a software engineer who spends most of my time thinking about how to make web experiences faster and more reliable. I'll share case studies from projects I've worked on, notes from things I've read, and rough ideas I'm still working through. Posts here won't always be polished — sometimes they'll be short and half-formed, because I think that's more honest than only publishing when something feels finished. If any of it is useful to you, that's a bonus.
